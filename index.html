Welcome to
Heartland
Timber Homes

At Heartland Timber Homes we use a patented manufacturing and assembly system that dramatically reduces the cost of your timber frame home — and the time it takes to erect it. Three people can erect a 1400’ home in just 9 days, without a crane.

An affordable
alternative to a
conventional house

You’ve always dreamed of owning a timber frame home. But your budget only allowed for an ordinary studwall and gyproc house. Now the game has changed. Now, you can own an authentic, stunning timber frame home at about the same cost as a conventional house.

Thinking of a milled
Log house? Move up
to Timber Frame.

If you’ve been shopping for a prefabricated milled log house or cabin, you can now enjoy the gracious warmth and beauty of an authentic timber frame home. This means you can avoid the “wall of pencils” look of most milled log houses — and enjoy the winter warmth and ambiance of solid timber. And we mean “solid timber”! The spaces between each post-and-beam frame contains horizontally stacked squared timbers. You can choose between 8″ and 10″ stock.

How HEARTLAND Timber Frame Homes are BUILT

Our affordable Timber Frame Home Building Packages Ship Worldwide

We produce affordable timber frame building packages for cabins, homes, and commercial buildings of all sizes. Our patented system reduces costs, not quality. We ship anywhere, and you can build anywhere with no crane needed.

Who We Are

We are in the Heart of BC’s timberlands. Partners Dave Loeks and Lindsay Flett have a lifelong love affair with wood. Their backgrounds in forestry, timber frame building, sawmilling and conservation are the basis for their revolutionary building system.

Our Process Is Simple

Whether it is a guest cabin or a luxury home, we start by discussing your ideas, then we draw up building plans, establish a firm price, and produce your HTH timber frame building package at our mill. Our beautiful, square timber system assembles quickly and without fuss. Get to lockup in record time; and enjoy your Heartland Timber Home for a lifetime.
